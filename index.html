Technological development is moving faster than ever and real life holographic 3D imaging is just around the corner. Meet Holoscreen; a new technology for 3D depth perception suited for all iterations of media and design. Holoscreen is a revolutionary screen technology that works just like any ordinary screen, but with the ability of unlocking an entire new dimension.
A screen technology that is bringing Imagination to Reality.

Compatible integration into ALL DEVICES.

We are spending approximately 45% of our awake time interacting with various screen bearing devices. But our devices are not yet an adequate extension of our everyday environment. We are hunting for higher resolutions, better audio quality, smarter functions, and a more intuitive user experience, while stuck with a virtual lifestyle in two dimensions. Therefore, it is time to break into the third dimension and blend the border between the real and the virtual.

Holoscreen, the new and innovative screen technology, is able to present a full fledged 3-dimensional experience. Expressing both viewer location parallax and depth perceptional stereopsis, Holoscreen resembles a life-like 3D environment identical to the impression of looking out a window!

Holoscreen can be produced both thin, cheap and in any number of sizes, making the integration into any future products easy. Even though the main advancement is Holoscreen’s ability to create a 3-dimensional image, it can still act as an ordinary 2-dimensional screen, and is compatible with touch technologies. Addition of gesture recognition and ultrasound haptic feedback would unlock the full 3-dimensional interactive experience and pave the way for an assortment of products that will present new and fresh approaches within design, presentation and media. A new and more natural way to experience the virtual world will then set the norm for future screen and display products in the same way colour support did in the 60s.
